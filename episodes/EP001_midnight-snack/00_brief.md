# EP001 — Bruno & Poppy: Midnight Snack Raid

- **POV headline (add in CapCut):** `POV: it's 2am and you both had the same idea`

    "just wanted water"`
- **Format:** 3D Pixar/chibi, wordless (non-verbal sounds only), third-person cute cinematic, ~24-28s
  final (4 scenes × ~8s raw, trimmed in CapCut)
- **Couple:** Bruno (big caramel-brown bear, green hoodie) + Poppy (small cream-white bunny, pink
  sweater) — same character sheet as every episode (`assets/characters/`)

- **The joke:** they both independently sneak out of bed at 2am for a snack and catch each other

- **The heart (why people share it):** "we're the same kind of gremlin" — the warm, funny
  togetherness of a couple caught in the exact same little habit.

## Content-safety check
Wholesome domestic couple comedy only: sneaking for a snack, getting caught, sharing food, a cuddle.
No danger, nothing scary (the 2am/dark setting stays cozy via warm fridge glow + soft moonlight —
handled in the positive description, never as a negative-prompt word). Generic unbranded snack
packaging and appliances. No real people. Clear.
